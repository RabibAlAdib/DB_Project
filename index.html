How is your baby doing?

Hope he is healthy and growing well. Maybe you have already given him a dose or two of vaccines. Your baby must get the required vaccines on time. This will protect him from many life-threatening diseases. Some vaccines are made available by the government free of cost while others are available for a cost in both private and government hospitals.

Required Vaccines

  • BCG -- Tuberculosis
  • Pentavalent -- Diphtheria, Pertussis, Tetanus, Hepatitis B, Haemophilus Influenza B
  • PCV -- Pneumococcal Pneumonia
  • OPV -- Poliomyelitis
  • MR -- Measles Rubella
  • Measles -- Measles
